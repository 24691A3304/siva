About AIMEX
AIMEX is the flagship technical fest of the Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Department, designed to ignite innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. The fest serves as a dynamic platform where technology meets imagination, bringing together students, innovators, and future technologists under one roof. AIMEX features a wide range of technical events including hackathons, coding challenges, AI model building, data science contests, and project expos that encourage real-time problem solving. Alongside technical events, the fest also hosts fun-filled games, workshops, quizzes, and interactive sessions to ensure an engaging experience for all participants.

About the Department
At MITS, Deemed to be University, the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is committed to nurturing future-ready engineers with strong technical and analytical skills. The program focuses on building a solid foundation in AI, ML, Data Science, Neural Networks, and Deep Learning. Students gain hands-on experience through advanced laboratories, real-time projects, internships, and industry-oriented curriculum.
About Our College
Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) is a prestigious Deemed to be University located in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh. The institution is recognized for its academic excellence, innovation-driven education, and strong research culture. MITS offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, management, and sciences. The university follows an outcome-based education system with a strong emphasis on practical learning and industry relevance. It is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced laboratories, smart classrooms, and modern research facilities. MITS has a highly qualified and experienced faculty who guide students toward academic and professional excellence.
